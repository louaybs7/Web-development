What’s Bmw M pack ?

BMW is an enormous manufacturer with one of the biggest ranges you’ll come across in your hunt for your next new car. If scouring their huge number of models isn’t already daunting enough, you’ll also find that each has a good few trims that you can pick from. These will include SE, Sport and – at the top of the range – M Sport, which gets the lion’s share of options and extra equipment.

However, just what is BMW M Sport?
Well, it’s a line named after BMW’s long-standing racing division, simply called ‘BMW M’, that promises to make your car more sporty-looking and luxurious. Getting the M Sport version of your chosen car can add around £3,000 to the price tag, so it’s a substantial step up, but is it one that’s right for you?

THE M SPORT PACKAGE: FOR A DYNAMIC APPEARANCE.

The M Sport package fascinates with dynamic details for BMW models, from wheels to paintwork, from exterior extras to interior comfort.
Exclusive M paints named after the world's most famous race circuits are a reference to our motorsports connection and are underscored by M light alloy wheels and the M aerodynamics package.
Inside, the theme is continued, with the M door sill finishers and roof liner in Anthracite. Comfortable M Sport seats provide lateral support, the M steering wheel provides perfect control and lets you feel the road beneath your wheels.
The optional M Sport package is available for almost all BMW automobiles. In-depth information regarding scope and details is available on the webpages about the specific vehicles and in the Configurator.

Experience the ultimate motorsport feeling with the Competition Package for the BMW M5 and BMW M6.
